## Summary of Key Changes

1. **Total baseline emissions increased** from 3,390 kton to 4,487 kton (+32.4% overall)
2. **Scope split changed** from 51.6% Scope 1+2 / 48.4% Scope 3 to 39.0% Scope 1+2 / 61.0% Scope 3
3. **Reduction percentages improved** for all scenarios due to higher baseline:
   - Amsterdam Goal: 30.7% → 47.7%
   - Dutch Goal: 19.4% → 39.1%
   - EAT-Lancet: 23.7% → 47.3%
   - Schijf van 5: 12.4% → 29.9%
   - Mediterranean: 16.2% → 37.0%
4. **Category emissions** are now dominated by Red Meat (2,462 kton) rather than Processed Meats
5. **Metropolitan risk scenario** shows smaller increase (+9.0%) rather than the large increase (+36.1%) previously reported
